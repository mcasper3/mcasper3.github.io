Hello! My name is Mike and I am currently a Junior in the Jeffrey S. Raikes School of Computer Science and Management at the University of Nebraska—Lincoln where I am studying computer science. Throughout my college career, I have had the opportunity to work on many exciting projects. In the near future, I am planning on beginning work on android tutorials aimed at beginners. These tutorials will be designed to introduce the basics in relation to an actual application as apposed to the most basic use-case.
